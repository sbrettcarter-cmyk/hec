You Can Help!

Homewood residents can promote environmental issues by participating in local events, spreading the word, contacting department heads and councilors, and making sustainable choices in their daily lives.

Reduce waste, use public transportation, support local sustainable businesses, and advocate for environmentally-friendly policies.

Ways You Can Help

Sustainability means meeting present needs without compromising future generations. It’s about balancing environmental health, economic vitality, and community well-being.

10 Easy Ways You Can Help

  • Contact local officials to support sustainability initiatives
  • Recycle properly and compost
  • Use energy-efficient appliances
  • Reduce water usage
  • Bike, walk, carpool, or use transit
  • Plant trees and native plants
  • Support sustainable local businesses
  • Reduce single-use plastics
  • Join community cleanups
  • Educate others

Top Invasive Plants

  1. English Ivy – aggressive vine damaging structures
  2. Chinese Wisteria – fast-growing invasive vine
  3. Chinese Privet – spreads rapidly through seeds and roots
  4. Taro – threatens waterways like the Cahaba River
